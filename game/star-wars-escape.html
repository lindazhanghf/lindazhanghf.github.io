Star Wars Escape

VR Room Escape Game


Demo Video

  • Built in Unity3D for Oculus Rift and Touch Controller
  • NPC AI and animations
  • Sound effects
  • Custom lighting and visual effects
  • Conducted user testing with 15+ people
